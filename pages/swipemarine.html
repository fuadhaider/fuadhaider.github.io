SwipeMarine


SwipeMarine is an endless runner mobile game made and published on Google Play in 2017. It was inspired from the famous mobile game Flappy Bird. Unlike tapping in Flappy Bird, SwipeMarine is unique for player needs to swipe to play the game.


I single-handedly made the whole game with

Unity 3D for game engine

C# for physics

Gimp for graphic design


Check it out on Google Play
